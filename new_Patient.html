36 Years of Dental Excellence

You don’t have to mirror somebody else’s smile. Our team will give you one that’s uniquely yours. We want you to have the smile that exudes your natural beauty.

Our team delights in treating our patients like the individuals that they are. We work hard on providing tailored dental care in a relaxing environment.

At Dr. Michael’s Dental Clinic, we feel strongly about protecting your oral health and your smile. We take great pride in taking a comprehensive and patient-friendly approach in all that we do. We believe that our committed level of preventive dentistry coupled with patient education is among the most effective ways of achieving Oral Health For Life.

Aside from the high standard of education they have acquired, our staff members have been compelled to undergo an extensive training in sterilization procedures.

All procedures at Dr. Michael's Dental Clinic are in accordance with the guidelines established by the Center for Disease Control (CDC) and the Occupational Safety and Health Agency (OSHA)..
